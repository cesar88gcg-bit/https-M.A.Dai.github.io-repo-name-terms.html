M.A.D ai

Multi-agent content and workflow operations

M.A.D ai is a locally managed operations platform for research, content drafting, workflow orchestration, review, scheduling, and approved third-party integrations.

The product is designed to support human-reviewed automation for ethical publishing, content operations, and business workflow management.

Terms of Service Privacy Policy

What it does

M.A.D ai helps operators manage AI-assisted workflows, including content generation, review pipelines, scheduling, research, and connected service actions approved by the account owner.

How it is used

Users connect approved services, review generated outputs, and decide what should be published, scheduled, or rejected. Human review remains central to the workflow.
